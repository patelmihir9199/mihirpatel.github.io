Problem Statement¶

What makes Family Guy animated show stand out from other animated comedy TV shows? The goals is to look at transcripts of various animated comedy TV shows and note their similarities and differences. Specifically, I'd like to know if Family Guy animated comedy show is different than other animated comedy TV shows, since it is the show that got me interested in animated comedy TV shows.

Data Gathering¶

To decide which animated comedy shows to look into, I went to IMDB and looked specifically at the best animated comedy TV shows. To narrow it down further, I looked only at those with greater than a 7/10 rating and more than 50,000 votes. The source was IMDB to find the top 10 popular animated comedy TV shows.

The list of top 10 animated comedy TV shows was sorted by Popularity:

  1. Family Guy
  2. The Simpsons
  3. Rick and Morty
  4. King of the Hill
  5. Bob's Burgers
  6. South Park
  7. BoJack Horseman
  8. American Dad!
  9. Archer
  10. Futurama

def url_to_transcript(url):
    page = requests.get(url).text
    soup = BeautifulSoup(page, "lxml")
    text = [p.text for p in soup.find(class_ = "postbody"'').find_all('p')]
    return text

def clean_text_round1(text):
    text = text.lower()
    text = re.sub('[.*?]', '', text)
    text = re.sub('[%s]' % re.escape(string.punctuation), '', text)
    text = re.sub('\w*\d\w*', '', text)
    return text

Document Term Matrix¶

Now, creating a document-term matrix that describes the frequency of terms that occur in each transcript of tv shows. We are using sckikit-learn CountVectorizer, where every row will represent a different document and every column will represent a different word.

At this step, we will remove the stop words like 'a', 'an', 'the', etc. as they make no additional meaning to text.
