Beyond my role at Dart Container, I am deeply passionate about the evolving fields of data science and artificial intelligence. These areas continually inspire me, as they hold the key to transforming the way we approach business challenges. I’m always eager to learn, experiment with new technologies, and explore innovative solutions to push the boundaries of what data can do.

With every project, my goal remains the same: to use data not just to answer questions, but to help businesses thrive and stay ahead of the curve in an increasingly data-driven world.
